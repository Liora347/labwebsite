## Research Overview

<div class="section">
<p>
The Meshorer Lab studies how <strong>chromatin structure and epigenetic mechanisms regulate cellular identity, development, and disease</strong>.
</p>

<p>
We combine experimental and computational approaches to explore genome plasticity in stem cells and neurons.
